Nyan Cat

About Nyan Cat

Nyan Cat is a YouTube video uploaded in April 2011, which became an internet meme.

The video merged a Japanese pop song with an animated cartoon cat with a Pop-Tart for a torso flying through space and leaving a rainbow trail behind.

The video ranked at number five on the list of most viewed YouTube videos in 2011.

What does Nyan Mean? Nyan is Japanese for "MEOW!".
Why does Nyan have a Pop-Tart body? The creator was livestreaming his drawings and his viewers
asked him to combine a Pop-Tart and a cat.
When was Nyan Cat Created? Apr 2nd, 2011
